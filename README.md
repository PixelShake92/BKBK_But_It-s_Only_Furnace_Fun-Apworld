# BK - Furnace Fun Only

> "What day is it? Oh? RIGHT!"

Congratulations. You clicked on this. Your life is about to get significantly worse in the best possible way.

This is an Archipelago randomizer mod for Banjo-Kazooie Recompiled that replaces the entire game with one single run of Furnace Fun. No worlds. No Mumbo. No notes. Just you, Grunty's quiz show from hell, and 77 tiles standing between you and sweet, sweet victory.

Good luck. You'll need it.

## How it works

The moment you load in, Bottles boots you straight to the Furnace Fun entrance. There is no escape. Every question tile on the board is an Archipelago location. Answer correctly, receive items, try not to land on a skull tile the ends up as a grunty question, repeat until tile 77 is done and you can finally go outside again.

## Items

| Item | Count | Effect |
|------|-------|--------|
| FF Board Access | 10 | Progressive — each one lets you reach deeper sections of the board. No access, no entry. This is shown by the black spaces. If you couldn't see the picture before walking on it you didn't have access. |
| Brentilda's Secrets #1-10 | 10 | Collect these to reveal the correct answer on Grunty Question tiles. Very useful. You can yell the correct answer if you see Brentildas face next to one of the answer options. |
| Filler (eggs, feathers, honeycombs, lives) | the rest | Standard BK consumables. These are fun too right? (But no I just needed some filler). |

## Locations

77 tile checks. All of them. Yes, including the skull tiles. Especially the skull tiles.

Progress is gated by how many FF Board Access items you've received — so if you're staring at a barrier and wondering why you can't proceed, that's not a bug, that's just your multiworld friends failing you.

## Notes

- Skull tiles will kill you instantly and send you back to the entrance. Your tile progress is saved. Your dignity is not.
- Brentilda hints only appear if you've actually received the corresponding Brentilda's Secrets item. Otherwise you're guessing. Have fun with that.
- Disconnecting mid-run and reconnecting will restore all your checked tiles from the server.
- This mod was made for April 1st. See here for more details https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/April_Fools%27_Day
